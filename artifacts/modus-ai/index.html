MODUS AI Associates — Malaysia

Building the World's First AI-Native Business and Lifestyle Operating System

MODUS AI Associates (legal name: Modus AI Associates LLP) is a Malaysian technology company building AI-native business infrastructure for Malaysian SMEs, the global halal economy, Southeast Asia and China. Microsoft builds Windows. Google builds Android. Modus builds AI-native infrastructure.

Who we are

MODUS AI Associates is Malaysia's gateway to China's AI ecosystem, delivering MIIT-certified AI training, enterprise AI transformation and a suite of integrated AI+ Super Apps comprising 35 AI subsystems. The company was founded on 17 December 2025 and is contactable at admin@modusaiassociates.com.

Our founder

Dato' Dr Tan Boon Nunt is the Founder & Director of MODUS AI Associates. He has been a pioneer of Malaysian e-commerce since 1994, was named Ernst & Young Technology Entrepreneur of the Year in 2002, and is a recipient of the Darjah Setia Pangkuan Negeri (D.S.P.N.) in 2022.
